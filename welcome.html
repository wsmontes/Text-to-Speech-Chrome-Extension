Welcome to Text-to-Speech Extension

Your browser can now read text aloud with a simple hover or selection

Getting Started

The extension is already active! Just hover over text to hear it spoken aloud.

You can also select text and press Alt+S to hear it.

Key Features

  • Automatic Language Detection - The extension will try to detect the language of text
  • Text Highlighting - See which text will be read
  • Site-Specific Settings - Enable or disable on specific websites
  • Customizable - Change voice speed, pitch, and more

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Alt+S - Speak selected text
  • Alt+T - Toggle settings panel

First Time Tips

  • Try to hover over various text elements on a page to see which ones trigger speech
  • Use the "Test Speech" button to verify your browser's audio is working properly
  • If you don't hear any speech, check the troubleshooting section in extension options
  • You can disable the extension on specific sites if you don't want it to activate there

If you encounter any issues, please visit the extension options page for troubleshooting.

Voice options and quality depend on your browser and operating system.
